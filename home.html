Fighting against the increase of energy consumption

The rising level of energy consumption that is occurring internationally is being mirrored at regional and national levels. An interesting case study along these lines is Tunisia, which is one of the high-growth economies in the Middle East and North African area yet lacks sufficient energy supply to satisfy its growing demand. Tunisia looks like many nations around the world with a young population, growing economy, increasing domestic energy consumption, and the need to balance economic development with environmental concerns.

We are concerned about the issue

Preserving the environment is the main goal of TechUp. Besides, we provide best quality photovoltaic cells and solar water heaters in order to reduce the need for non-renewable energy sources. The agricultural technologies we offer also contribute to the protection of fertile soil.

It is our duty to tech green.
